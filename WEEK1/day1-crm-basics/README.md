# Day 1 - CRM Basics

## What is CRM?
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.
It helps companies manage customer information,
sales processes, and communication.

---

## Why Companies Use Salesforce
Companies use Salesforce to:
- manage customers,
- track sales,
- automate workflows,
- improve business efficiency.

---

## Salesforce Objects

### Account
Represents a company or organization.

### Contact
Represents a person associated with an account.

### Opportunity
Represents a possible business deal or sales process.

---

## Real-World Mapping (Example: College Admission)

Account = College

Contact = Student

Lead = Student interested in admission

Opportunity = Admission process

Customer = Enrolled student
